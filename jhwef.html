PROJECT SHIKSHA

Events always play a crucial role in a foundation because they showcase the marketing and network of the foundation. Events show the value of your foundation, making people more engaged, which increases endorsements and awareness. Events create awareness about children, encouraging people to connect more. By organizing events, the foundation raises awareness and inspires people to take potential steps for children. Events aim to create a positive change in the environment, helping the foundation expand its network, marketing, and engagement efforts.
